About Me

Hi, I’m Hana Nigus, a third-year Computer Science student at Wolaita Sodo University. I was born and raised in Addis Ababa, and I’m passionate about technology, creativity, and building meaningful things. My goal is to become a full-stack developer who creates smart and impactful digital experiences.

Alongside coding, I also run a small business where I design and produce plastic kavana bags blending innovation, sustainability, and style. I believe the future belongs to those who create it, and I’m excited to keep learning, growing, and building towards mine.
